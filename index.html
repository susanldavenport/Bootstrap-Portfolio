About Me

For the past seven years I have served as the Practice Manager of Aesthetic and Implant Dentistry of Atlanta, a cosmetic and dental implant office in North Atlanta. Recently, I became a student of Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp, where I am learning to be a Full Stack Developer in the hopes of beginning a successful career in Web Development.


I have lived in Atlanta for over 11 years with my boyfriend and my pommeranian, Bella. My hobbies vary widely and include hiking, snow and wakeboarding, boating, reading, and studying the tarot. I am passionate about coding, cooking, yoga, and interior design.
